Detailed Instruction

CLICK ON THE START BUTTON,to start the sign language recognition to speech,If lagging occurs with the camera screen click on START BUTTON

>>Press "Enter"on your keyboard to save the text recognised for converting to speech.

>>Press "Space Bar" to leave a space.

>>Click "Backspace" key on your keyboard to delete a character from right to left.

>>Click "Delete" to delete entire text.

>> The Saved text appears on the top left corner of the video Screen

>> Once you are satisfied with the saved text press"STOP" to convert it into speech

>>NOTE: The hand must be on the screen to display the text to save,delete or to leave a space between them.
