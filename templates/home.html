About SLR

SLR is a powerful web app that utilizes smachine learning algorithms, to recognize and interpret sign language gestures accurately...

Features

The SLR system is capable of recognizing sign language gestures in real-time to provide a seamless user experience. It interprets the gestures quickly and accurately, allowing the web app to respond accordingly.

By incorporating SLR into a web app, it enhances accessibility for individuals who use sign language as their primary means of communication. It enables deaf and hard-of-hearing users to interact with the app on an equal footing with non-sign language users
